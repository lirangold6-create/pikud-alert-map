🔑 Key Visual Signature

The system detects multi-missile attacks by identifying separated red polygons with gaps between them. On average, 27% of orange alerts fall in the gap zone between clusters. This spatial separation (typically 50-80km) is the strongest indicator of multiple simultaneous missile impacts.
